Navigate the Ontario School Map

Below you'll find some instructions to help you utilize the features of our map:

  • Utilize the map interface to select either or both Elementary or Secondary school location.
  • Choose a specific year of interest from the map's dropdown menu.
  • Use the zoom function to adjust the map view and navigate to your desired location.
  • Click on a school marker to access detailed information about the selected institution.
